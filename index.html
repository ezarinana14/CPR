Why Should You Become Certified?

Life is full of surpises and sometimes those surprises take the form of emergencies. Learning CPR and First Aid can prepare you in responding to various situations such as choking, burns, bleeding, heart attacks, strokes and other emergencies.

HEART THINK CPR provides fun, easy to learn instruction that will effectively teach you the steps, techniques and confidence needed to respond to emergencies and save a life.

Classes can be taught anywhere you would like, whether it be the comfort of your own home, school, church, child care facility or any other setting that is most convenient.

Instructor

Eduardo Zarinana is an American Red Cross Certified First Aid, CPR and AED Instructor. Taking an energetic and engaging approach, Eduardo will teach you that as intimidating and serious as CPR and First Aid may seem, the path to becoming certified can be enjoybale and valuable as well as rewarding in the long run. The use of videos, scenarios, and hands on practice will assist you in retaining what you will learn throughout the course.
